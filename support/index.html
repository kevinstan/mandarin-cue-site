Support

Mandarin Cue Support

Mandarin Cue is a practical Mandarin micro-practice app for iPhone widgets, useful phrases, and tiny review sessions.

Contact

For app issues, purchase questions, feedback, or feature requests, email:

support@mandarincue.app

Please include the app version, your iOS version, whether the issue happened in the app or widget, and a short description of what you expected and what happened.

Purchases

Mandarin Cue Pro purchases and restore purchases are handled through Apple StoreKit. If a purchase does not appear, open Settings in Mandarin Cue and use Restore Purchases.

Privacy

Mandarin Cue does not require an account in v1. Learner progress is stored locally on your device. Widget data is shared only through the app group so widgets can show compact cue snapshots.

Widgets

Widget taps should open the exact same cue in the app. If a widget opens the wrong cue, email support with the card shown on the widget and the card opened in the app.
